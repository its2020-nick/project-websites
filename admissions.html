Admissions

Join our growing family — learn how to enroll

Home Admissions
We welcome applications for all learning levels — from Pre-Primary through Junior Secondary. Enrollment is open throughout the year, subject to availability.

Admission Requirements

Age Requirements

PP1: 4 years · PP2: 5 years · Grade 1: 6 years · Grades 7–9: as per KICD guidelines

Birth Certificate

Certified copy of the learner's birth certificate (original for verification)

Passport Photos

2 recent passport-size colour photographs of the learner

Medical Records

Up-to-date immunisation / vaccination card (for PP1 & PP2)

Previous School Records

Most recent school report (for Grades 2–9 transfers)

Parent / Guardian ID

Copy of National ID or Passport of the parent or legal guardian

How to Apply

1

Visit the School

Come to our office at Mwiki Road, Kasarani during office hours (Mon–Fri, 7:00 AM–5:00 PM).

2

Collect Form

Pick up an application form from the school secretary or download it from our contact page.

3

Submit Documents

Complete the form and submit it together with all required documents listed above.

4

Assessment (if applicable)

Learners joining Grade 4 and above may be asked to sit a short assessment to determine the appropriate class level.

5

Admission Offer

You will be notified within 3–5 working days. Upon acceptance, you will receive a joining instructions letter.

6

Pay Fees & Report

Settle the required fees as per the fee structure and report on the agreed start date.

Fee Schedule Available on Request

Frequently Asked Questions

We currently do not operate a dedicated school bus. However, we can advise on matatu routes serving the Mwiki and Kasarani area that are commonly used by our learners. Please inquire at the office for more information.

We fully implement Kenya's Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) as developed by the Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development (KICD). CBC covers Pre-Primary, Lower Primary (Grades 1–3), Upper Primary (Grades 4–6), and Junior Secondary (Grades 7–9). Visit our Curriculum page to learn more.
