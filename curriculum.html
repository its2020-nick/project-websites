About Our Curriculum

Wekan Kasarani School implements Kenya's Competency Based Curriculum (CBC), introduced by the Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development (KICD).

Learning Levels

Pre-Primary — PP1 & PP2 (Ages 4–5)

The Pre-Primary level focuses on play-based learning to develop foundational literacy, numeracy, and social skills. Learners are introduced to the world through exploration and creativity.

Language Activities (English)
Language Activities (Kiswahili)
Mathematical Activities
Environmental Activities
Creative Activities
Psychomotor & Creative Arts
Religious Activities (CRE/IRE/HRE)

Lower Primary — Grades 1–3 (Ages 6–8)

Learners build core literacy and numeracy skills while developing scientific, social, and creative thinking. Kiswahili is used as the language of instruction in early grades.

Literacy Activities (English)
Kiswahili (Kusoma na Kuandika)
Mathematical Activities
Environmental & Social Studies
Science & Technology
Creative Arts & Sports
Religious Education (CRE/IRE/HRE)
Social Skills

Upper Primary — Grades 4–6 (Ages 9–11)

Learners deepen subject knowledge and begin to apply skills in real-world contexts. Emphasis is placed on critical thinking, problem solving, and STEM fundamentals.

English
Kiswahili
Mathematics
Integrated Science
Social Studies
Agriculture & Nutrition
Creative Arts & Sports
Religious Education (CRE/IRE/HRE)
Pre-Technical & Pre-Career Education

Junior Secondary — Grades 7–9 (Ages 12–14)

Junior Secondary expands into distinct subject areas. Learners are guided to identify and develop their talents and career interests through a broad curriculum.
